📖 RULES
🎯 Goal
Your main objective is to climb as high as possible on the leaderboard by setting stronger and stronger run scores.

Coins are not the final goal — they are your resource to buy Store upgrades that help you survive longer, score faster, and keep pushing to higher leaderboard positions.
🕹️ Controls
Swipe Left / Right — change lanes
Swipe Up — spin (dodge obstacles, has cooldown)
Desktop: Arrow keys ← → to move, ↑ or Space to spin
🎮 How to Play
Your character automatically runs forward at increasing speed. Navigate between 3 lanes to collect silver and gold coins, pick up bonuses, and dodge obstacles like fences, rocks, and bulls.

Silver coins = +10 score
Gold coins = +100 score

Hit an obstacle and your run ends — unless you have a 🛡 Shield!
⚡ Power-ups
🛡 Shield — blocks one hit
✖2 Score — doubles all points for a limited time
🧲 Magnet — attracts nearby coins
⚡ Speed Up — boosts your speed (more distance!)
🐌 Speed Down — slows you down (easier to dodge)
➕ Score +300 — instant score bonus
➕ Score +500 — bigger instant score bonus
➖ Score -300 — instant score penalty
➖ Score -500 — heavy score penalty
🔄 Invert — inverts your controls temporarily
🔄 Recharge — resets your spin cooldown
🛒 Store & Upgrades
Spend your earned coins in the Store (available from the main menu after connecting a wallet).

Silver upgrades (3 tiers each):
✖2 Score duration, Score +300 multiplier, Score +500 multiplier, Score -300 reduction, Score -500 reduction, Invert score multiplier, Speed Up multiplier, Speed Down multiplier, Magnet duration, Spin cooldown reduction.

Gold upgrades:
🛡 Shield Start (start rides with shield),
🛡 Shield Stack (increase max shields to 2/3),
📡 Radar (highlights lane before gold coin),
🌀 Spin Alert / Perfect Spin (warnings and stronger ring timing support),
🎟 Rides Pack (+3 rides)
🎟 Rides System
You get 3 free rides that refill every 8 hours. When you're out, you can purchase extra ride packs with gold coins in the Store.
