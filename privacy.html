Privacy Policy — Ursass Tube

Last updated: April 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Ursass Tube collects, uses, stores, and protects user information when you use our Telegram Mini App and related bot.

Ursass Tube is an arcade runner game available through Telegram. The current version of the product is focused only on the Ursass Tube game experience: gameplay, score tracking, leaderboard, tasks, and related in-game features.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Telegram account information

When you open Ursass Tube through Telegram, Telegram may provide us with limited account information, such as:

  • Telegram user ID;
  • username;
  • first name;
  • profile photo, if available;
  • language code;
  • Telegram Mini App launch data.

We use this information to identify your game profile, save your progress, and display your result in the leaderboard.

1.2 Game data

We may collect and store game-related data, including:

  • current score;
  • best score;
  • leaderboard position;
  • coins or other in-game progress;
  • completed tasks;
  • ride/session history;
  • referral or invite activity, if used;
  • gameplay events needed for analytics and anti-cheat protection.

1.3 Technical data

We may collect technical information needed to operate and improve the game, such as:

  • device type;
  • operating system;
  • browser or Telegram client information;
  • app version;
  • error logs;
  • session timestamps;
  • approximate performance data.

1.4 Analytics data

We may collect basic analytics events, such as:

  • app opened;
  • game started;
  • game finished;
  • score submitted;
  • leaderboard opened;
  • task completed;
  • share button clicked.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to provide access to the game; create and maintain your game profile; save your progress and scores; display leaderboard results; prevent cheating, abuse, and fake score submissions; improve gameplay, performance, and user experience; fix bugs and technical issues; analyze product usage; and communicate important product updates through the Telegram bot, where permitted.

3. Leaderboard and Public Game Data

If you submit a score, some information may be visible to other users in the leaderboard, such as username or display name, score, rank, and best result.

Do not use Ursass Tube if you do not want your public game result to appear in the leaderboard.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share limited information only when necessary: with infrastructure providers used to host and operate the game; with analytics or error-monitoring tools; if required by law or legal process; to protect the game from fraud, cheating, abuse, or security threats.

5. Telegram Platform

Ursass Tube operates as a Telegram Mini App. Your use of Telegram is also governed by Telegram’s own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

We are responsible only for the data processed by Ursass Tube. We do not control how Telegram processes your data.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data.

Telegram Mini App launch data is validated on the server before being used for authentication or score-related features. We do not rely only on unsafe client-side data.

However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection against all risks.

7. Data Retention

We keep user data only as long as needed to provide the game; maintain leaderboards; prevent abuse; analyze and improve the product; and comply with legal obligations.

We may delete inactive accounts, old gameplay sessions, or outdated technical logs at our discretion.

8. User Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to request access to your data, request correction of inaccurate data, request deletion of your data, object to certain processing, and request limitation of processing.

To make a request, contact us using the contact details below.

9. Children

Ursass Tube is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law to use online services without parental consent.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we may notify users through the Telegram bot, the Mini App, or our website.

Your continued use of Ursass Tube after changes means that you accept the updated Privacy Policy.
